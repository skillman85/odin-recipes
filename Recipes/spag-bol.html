Spaghetti Bolognese

Spaghetti Bolognese is a classic Italian meat sauce that is a staple in most families. My super simple Spaghetti Bolognese is the perfect dinner for any night of the week and will wow your family or guests.

Ingredients

  • basil
  • pasta
  • oregano
  • pepper
  • Pasta sauce
  • Mushrooms
  • Peppers
  • Red Wine

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Heat the olive oil in a large skillet or pot over medium-high heat. Add the ground beef and cook for 3 minutes, breaking it as you go, until no longer pink.
  2. Add veggies, spices and wine: Add the onion and garlic and cook for another 3 minutes until the onion softens. Add the oregano, red pepper flakes, tomato paste and stir. Stir in the red wine and cook for about 5 minutes until most of the liquid has cooked off.
  3. In goes the crushed tomatoes: Add the crushed tomatoes, basil and season with salt and pepper. Stir and bring to a boil, then reduce the heat to a medium-low. Cover the pot and cook for about 20 minutes
  4. Finish the sauce: Stir in the heavy cream and adjust for seasoning if needed. If your sauce is too thick add some of the pasta water to thin it out.
